Digital Literacy

Gain essential digital skills to navigate the online world safely, effectively, and responsibly.

Course Overview

This course introduces learners to the fundamental skills required in today’s digital age. It covers computer basics, internet usage, online safety, and productivity tools, enabling learners to thrive in education, work, and everyday life.

Duration
8 Weeks
Level
Beginner to Intermediate
Mode
Online / Offline
Language
Multilingual

Course Content

  • Introduction to computers & devices
  • Basic internet skills & online communication
  • Email, cloud storage, and collaboration tools
  • Cybersecurity and online safety
  • Using productivity tools (Word, Excel, Presentations)
  • Digital citizenship and responsible online behavior

Learning Outcomes

  • Confidently use computers and digital devices
  • Communicate effectively online
  • Stay safe and secure in the digital world
  • Use productivity tools for work and study
  • Develop responsible and ethical online behavior
