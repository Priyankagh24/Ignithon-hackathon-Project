Environmental Science

Explore the interactions between humans and the environment while learning sustainable practices to protect our planet.

Course Overview

This course introduces the principles of environmental science, focusing on ecosystems, natural resources, sustainability, and human impact on the environment. Students will engage in practical learning and case studies to understand real-world environmental challenges.

Duration
10 Weeks
Level
Beginner to Intermediate
Mode
Online / Offline
Language
Multilingual

Course Content

  • Introduction to environmental science
  • Ecosystems and biodiversity
  • Natural resources and conservation
  • Pollution and climate change
  • Sustainable development practices
  • Case studies on environmental issues

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the relationship between humans and the environment
  • Recognize the importance of biodiversity and ecosystems
  • Identify major environmental challenges and solutions
  • Apply sustainable practices in daily life
  • Develop awareness to contribute to environmental protection
