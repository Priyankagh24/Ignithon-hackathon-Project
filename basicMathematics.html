Basic Mathematics

Build a strong foundation in mathematics with real-world applications and interactive learning.

Course Overview

This course covers fundamental math concepts needed in daily life and academics. It uses local examples and practical problem-solving approaches to make learning meaningful and fun.

Duration
6 Weeks
Level
Beginner
Mode
Online / Offline
Language
Multilingual

Course Content

  • Basic arithmetic operations
  • Introduction to algebra
  • Geometry basics
  • Fractions, decimals & percentages
  • Word problems and applications

Learning Outcomes

  • Perform basic arithmetic with confidence
  • Apply algebra to solve simple equations
  • Understand shapes, space, and measurements
  • Use mathematics in everyday problem-solving
