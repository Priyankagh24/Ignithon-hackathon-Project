Entrepreneurship

Learn how to turn ideas into successful ventures with practical tools, strategies, and real-world examples.

Course Overview

This course introduces the fundamentals of entrepreneurship, from developing innovative ideas to building sustainable businesses. It focuses on practical approaches, case studies, and local entrepreneurial success stories.

Duration
8 Weeks
Level
Beginner to Intermediate
Mode
Online / Offline
Language
Multilingual

Course Content

  • Introduction to entrepreneurship
  • Idea generation and validation
  • Business models and planning
  • Marketing and customer acquisition
  • Finance and funding basics
  • Case studies and real-world applications

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the core principles of entrepreneurship
  • Develop and evaluate business ideas
  • Create a basic business plan
  • Gain knowledge of marketing and financial management
  • Build confidence to start and sustain a venture
